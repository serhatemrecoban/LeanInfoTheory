This hand-written map will be replaced by a generated blueprint dependency graph once the semantic bridge theorems start landing.

  1. LeanInfoTheory.Basic: lightweight project names.
  2. LeanInfoTheory.Probability.Finite: real-valued finite-mass lemmas for PMFs.
  3. LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.Entropy: finite Shannon entropy over mathlib PMFs, including relabeling invariance.
  4. LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.InfoMeasures: marginals, entropy-derived information measures, support lemmas, and orientation lemmas.
  5. LeanInfoTheory.InformationMeasures: public finite information-measure API.
  6. LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge: future KL-divergence and conditional-law bridge layer.
  7. LeanInfoTheory.EntropyExpr: defines formal entropy atoms and rational expressions.
  8. LeanInfoTheory.EntropyVal: packages abstract Shannon entropy valuation assumptions.
  9. LeanInfoTheory.PrimitiveIneq: defines primitive Shannon inequality expressions and proves their valuation soundness.
  10. LeanInfoTheory.Certificate: proves nonnegative-combination certificate soundness and exact decomposition matching.
  11. LeanInfoTheory.Certificate.Checked: separates raw certificates from proof-carrying checked certificates, with nonnegative rational coefficients, exact decomposition equality, and a first raw-to-checked validator.
  12. LeanInfoTheory.Certificate.Submodularity: proves entropy submodularity through a validated CMI certificate.
  13. LeanInfoTheory.Examples: contains closed toy examples for the certificate layers.
