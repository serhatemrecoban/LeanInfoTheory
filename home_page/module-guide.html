Module Guide

Current compileable Lean modules and how they fit together.

This is the hand-written companion to the generated declaration index. Full Lean doc-gen output is still a later milestone.

  • LeanInfoTheory.Basic: lightweight project namespace and shared status vocabulary.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Probability.Finite: real-mass bridge lemmas and pointwise PMF.map facts for finite PMF sums and relabeling arguments.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Probability.FiniteChannel: opt-in, type-generic PMF-valued channel constructions and their atom, projection, composition, deterministic-map, and support laws.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.Entropy: finite Shannon entropy, random-variable entropy, zero-entropy characterizations, and relabeling invariance theorems.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.EntropyBounds: Jensen-based alphabet and support-cardinality upper bounds plus the uniform-law equality theorem. Import this module explicitly when those heavier analytic bounds are needed.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.InfoMeasures: marginals, conditional entropy, mutual information, conditional mutual information, chain rules, symmetry, and textbook entropy identities.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.Units: opt-in logarithm-base conversion; canonical information measures remain in nats.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge: separated bridge entry point for expected self-information, finite conditional laws, KL formulas, independence and Markov semantics, stochastic data processing, and pair/triple inequality theorems.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge.Product: independent-product PMF infrastructure, product-measure bridge lemmas, and joint-law absolute-continuity facts.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge.FiniteSums: finite real-sum rewrites for marginals, entropy, and the log-ratio formula for mutual information.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge.Conditional: finite conditional-law API and expected conditional entropy / conditional mutual information fiber formulas.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge.KL: finite KL expansion, mutual information as KL, and averaged conditional-KL bridge theorems.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge.Independence: ordinary and conditional independence predicates, mathlib bridges, positive-fiber formulations, and zero-MI/zero-CMI characterizations.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge.Markov: finite Markov predicates and characterizations, total conditional channels, canonical/existential channel factorization, exact information loss, and MI data processing.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge.DataProcessing: PMF-channel-to-kernel and posterior bridges, exact KL decomposition, finite KL contraction, invariant-law consequences, and doubly stochastic entropy growth.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Shannon.SemanticBridge.Theorems: user-facing semantic theorems, including nonnegativity, zero conditional entropy, deterministic entropy/MI processing, chain-rule variants, and ordinary/conditional inequality bands.
  • LeanInfoTheory.InformationMeasures: public convenience re-export for the finite information-measure API; theorem-oriented code should usually prefer the canonical LeanInfoTheory.Shannon names.
  • LeanInfoTheory.EntropyExpr: formal entropy-expression algebra and the empty-entropy convention interface.
  • LeanInfoTheory.EntropyVal: abstract Shannon entropy valuations for certificate work.
  • LeanInfoTheory.PrimitiveIneq: primitive Shannon inequality expressions and their soundness under abstract valuations.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Certificate: generic nonnegative-combination soundness theorem and exact decomposition matching.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Certificate.Checked: raw certificate data, a first raw-to-checked validator, and proof-carrying checked certificate structures with nonnegative rational coefficients and exact decomposition equality.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Certificate.Submodularity: separately importable first non-toy certificate demo, proving entropy submodularity from a validated CMI certificate.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Certificate.Subadditivity: separately importable checked certificate demo, proving entropy subadditivity from a two-step primitive certificate.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Certificate.Monotonicity: separately importable checked certificate demo, proving one-variable entropy monotonicity from a conditional-entropy primitive.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Certificate.ThreeWaySubadditivity: separately importable manual certificate pressure-test module, proving three-way entropy subadditivity from a four-step primitive certificate.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Examples.CommonCause: opt-in noisy binary common-cause example exercising conditional independence, zero CMI, and Markov factorization.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Examples.StochasticChannels: opt-in stochastic reset/cascade examples exercising KL contraction, support-sensitive real bounds, and strict entropy growth.
  • LeanInfoTheory.Examples: aggregate import for the separately importable certificate and semantic examples.

Human-facing design notes are tracked in docs/foundation-conventions.md and docs/project-log.md in the repository.
