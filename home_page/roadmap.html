Roadmap

A 3 / 6 / 12 month plan for the library and certificate checker.

Home Blueprint Documentation

Now

  • Initialize the Lake/mathlib project.
  • Add honest public positioning and website.
  • Add algebraic entropy-expression primitives.
  • Add at least one closed theorem in the certificate layer.

3 months

  • Audit mathlib, PFR, and related divergence formalizations.
  • Decide the concrete finite entropy representation.
  • Prove basic expression-normalization lemmas.
  • Implement a small hand-written certificate checker.

6 months

  • Connect the semantic layer to concrete entropy/MI/CMI definitions.
  • Prove elemental inequality soundness for the checker.
  • Certify several textbook entropy inequalities.
  • Prepare focused mathlib PRs for generic finite-measure lemmas.

12 months

  • Add PSITIP/oXitip-style certificate import.
  • Formalize selected converse proof skeletons.
  • Write a technical report or short paper describing the Lean library and certificate pipeline.
